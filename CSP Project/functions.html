Our Café provides the ideal function venue, specializing in quality modern cuisine designed to individual requirements. Our great setting combined with relaxed and professional service makes our café a must for your next event.

Catering

This fashionable venue can cater for any special occasion, up to 32 guests seated and can accommodate a 40 person stand up event such as a birthday celebration, cocktail party, or other event. Groups of 10 or more must decide on a set menu option. BYO is not available for group and event bookings. Fat Jack's Café is happy to tailor your event to suit your needs and price range.
